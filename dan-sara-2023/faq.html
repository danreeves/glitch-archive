Where can I stay?

We have not booked any rooms/hotel so that people can plan out their London stay as it suits them best!

The venue itself is in Camden Town where there are plenty of transport link connections so feel free to stay in an area of London that looks the most interesting/has accomodations that work for you!

Camden Town station (on the Northern Line) is a 5 minute walk from the venue. Camden Road overground station is also just a few minutes' walk away! There are plenty of buses and two major train stations (Euston and St.Pancras/King's Cross) within a 15-20 minute walk!

Do you have a registry?

We don't have a registry! You don't have to get us any gifts! Celebrating together is what we want to do most.

Where do I share dietary restrictions?

Closer to the event date the team at the venue will send out a form for you to fill and pre-order your meal! Please let them know if you need anything.
